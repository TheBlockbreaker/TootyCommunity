TEAM SELECTION

Lobby based team selector which has a balance check to en-sure teams are balanced

print('Team Selection!')
This is a old project I did a while back part as a game I was making with some friends. It features toast notifications and a team-selection system where you walk up to a room of the team and you'll be teleported inside if the teams are balanced. Once enough players on either side, it will count down from 10 then teleport the players to the main area of the map.
